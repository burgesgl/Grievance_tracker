## How to Use

1. **Add a New Grievance**: Click the "+ New Grievance" button
2. **Fill in Details**: Enter grievant name, issue/violation, status, priority, and other relevant information
3. **Attach Files**: Drag and drop or click to upload supporting documents, emails, photos, or PDFs
4. **Generate Documents**: Use built-in templates to create grievance forms, formal letters, or appeals
5. **Track Progress**: Update status as the grievance moves through investigation, mediation, or arbitration
6. **Filter and Search**: Use the filter controls to find specific grievances quickly
7. **Export Data**: Click "Export CSV" to download your grievance data

## Document Generation

The tracker includes three built-in templates:

1. **Basic Grievance Form**: Standard grievance documentation
2. **Formal Grievance Letter**: Professional letter format for official filing
3. **Appeal Form**: Template for appealing grievance decisions

All templates automatically populate with your grievance data and can be customized.

## File Management

- **Upload Files**: Drag and drop or click to upload documents
- **Supported Formats**: PDF, Word documents, images (JPG, PNG), text files
- **File Storage**: Files are stored securely in your browser
- **View & Download**: Access attached files anytime from the grievance details

## Statuses

- **Filed**: Initial grievance filed
- **Investigation**: Under investigation
- **Mediation**: In mediation process
- **Arbitration**: Escalated to arbitration
- **Resolved**: Successfully resolved
- **Withdrawn**: Grievance withdrawn

## Priority Levels

- **High**: Urgent cases requiring immediate attention
- **Medium**: Standard priority cases
- **Low**: Lower priority or monitoring cases
